# NexusBar

**NexusBar is a modern Windows launcher designed to keep your desktop organized and your favorite apps just a click away.**

Instead of cluttering your taskbar with shortcuts, NexusBar gives you a clean, customizable hub where you can organize applications, folders, and websites into categories that fit your workflow. With a sleek dark interface, global search, favorites, custom icons, themes, and quick access features, NexusBar aims to make launching what you need both fast and enjoyable.

Built with simplicity and personalization in mind, NexusBar lets you create a launcher that feels uniquely yours—whether you're organizing games, productivity tools, development software, or everyday essentials.

### Features

* 🔍 Instant search and quick launch
* ⭐ Favorites for your most-used apps
* 📁 Support for applications, folders, and websites
* 🎨 Custom themes and accent colors
* 🖼️ Custom app icons
* 🗂️ Categories to keep everything organized
* ⌨️ Global hotkey support
* 💾 Import and export your layouts
* 🔄 Built-in update support
* ✨ Clean, modern interface inspired by today's best desktop experiences

**Your apps. One place.**
